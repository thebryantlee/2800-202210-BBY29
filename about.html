Meet the team of BBY-29

Brought together by the Computer Systems Technology Program at the British Columbia Institute of Technology, BBY-29 was formed to create a project for their COMP 2800 course. We bonded over a shared interest in computers, gaming, and saving money. We're devoted to making it easier for people of all ages and backgrounds to create an affordable computer that fits their needs.
